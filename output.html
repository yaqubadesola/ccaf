# One-Day Vegan Meal Plan for Soccer Performance

Here is a one-day meal plan for a vegan athlete preparing for an evening competitive soccer match:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 2,800 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (105g), Fat (85g), Carbs (385g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oatmeal (80g dry weight) with banana (120g), ground flaxseeds (15g), and unsweetened almond milk (240g). Side of whole grain toast (50g) with almond butter (20g).
  * Protein: 18g, Fat: 16g, Carbs: 75g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM):** Smoothie bowl with plant-based protein powder (30g), frozen berries (150g), coconut yogurt (150g), and granola (40g).
  * Protein: 22g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 65g

  * Protein: 18g, Fat: 16g, Carbs: 95g

* **Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):** Apple (180g) with peanut butter (25g) and a handful of raw almonds (20g).
  * Protein: 10g, Fat: 20g, Carbs: 35g

* **Pre-Match Dinner (6:00 PM):** Pasta (150g cooked) with marinara sauce (200g) made with lentils (75g cooked), garlic, and herbs. Steamed broccoli (150g) with nutritional yeast (10g). Light olive oil (10g).
  * Protein: 24g, Fat: 14g, Carbs: 105g

* **Evening Snack (30 minutes before match, 7:30 PM):** Energy bars (2 x 40g each) made with dates, oats, and nuts, plus electrolyte sports drink (500g).
  * Protein: 13g, Fat: 7g, Carbs: 110g
